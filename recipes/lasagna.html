Lasagna

Description

Recipe for meat lasagna with homemade tomato sauce and cheese filling.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Noodles
  • cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
