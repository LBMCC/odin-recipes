French Fries

Description

Recipe for seasoned french fries.

Ingredients

  • 2 half pounds russet potatoes, peeled
  • 1 cup vegetable oil for frying
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1 teaspoon onion salt
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • half a cup water, or as needed
