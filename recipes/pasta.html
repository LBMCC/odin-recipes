Pasta

Description

Recipe for easy to make homemade pasta.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Make the dough
  2. Turn the dough out and knead it for 10 minutes
  3. Let the dough rest
  4. Divide the dough into balls
  5. Use a pasta machine to roll and cut the dough into desired shapes
  6. Boil water, salt it and then add the pasta
  7. Cook the pasta until it's al dente
